Frequently Asked Questions

When is the application deadline?

We accept applications year-round, but we recommend applying by March 31st for the following academic year to ensure availability and early bird discounts.

Do you accept mid-year transfers?

Yes, we accept mid-year transfers subject to availability. Please contact our admissions office to discuss your specific situation.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

Our student-teacher ratios are: Early Years (8:1), Primary (12:1), Middle School (15:1), and Senior School (18:1).

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we offer safe and reliable bus transportation covering major areas of the city. Transportation fees are separate from tuition.
