Nurturing curious minds, building confident leaders, creating compassionate global citizens

Our Story

Founded in 2010, BoosTrack School began with a simple yet profound vision: to create an educational environment where every child could discover their unique potential and develop into confident, compassionate global citizens.

What started as a small community of dedicated educators and forward-thinking families has grown into one of the region's most respected international schools. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to excellence, and a deep understanding that education is not just about academic achievementâ€”it's about nurturing the whole child.

Today, we proudly serve over 800 students from 40+ nationalities, offering world-class Cambridge and International Baccalaureate programs from Early Years through to university preparation. Our graduates have gone on to attend prestigious universities worldwide, but more importantly, they carry with them the values, skills, and global perspective needed to make a positive impact in our interconnected world.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional international education that empowers students to become confident, creative, and compassionate global citizens who contribute positively to their communities and the world.

  • Foster intellectual curiosity and critical thinking
  • Develop strong character and ethical values
  • Cultivate global awareness and cultural understanding
  • Encourage innovation and creativity

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading international school that inspires lifelong learning, nurtures individual potential, and prepares students to thrive in an ever-changing global landscape.

  • Excellence in teaching and learning
  • Innovation in educational practices
  • Strong community partnerships
  • Sustainable and responsible citizenship

Our Values

Our core values guide every decision we make and every interaction we have within our school community.

  • Integrity: Acting with honesty and moral principles
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards
  • Respect: Valuing diversity and treating others with dignity
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and new ideas
  • Community: Building strong, supportive relationships

Our Educational Philosophy

Holistic education that develops the whole child

Student-Centered Learning

We believe that every child is unique, with their own learning style, interests, and potential. Our approach is centered on understanding each student as an individual and tailoring our teaching methods to help them achieve their personal best.

Global Perspective

In our interconnected world, we prepare students to be global citizens who understand and appreciate different cultures, perspectives, and ways of thinking. Our international curriculum and diverse community provide the perfect environment for developing this global mindset.

Character Development

Academic excellence is just one part of education. We place equal emphasis on developing character, values, and social-emotional skills that will serve our students throughout their lives.

Innovation and Creativity

We encourage students to think creatively, question assumptions, and approach problems with innovative solutions. Our learning environments are designed to foster curiosity, experimentation, and creative expression.
